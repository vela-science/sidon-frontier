Confidence policy for the Sidon-set additive-combinatorics frontier.

Confidence changes must distinguish established theorem, surveyed result,
finite computation, heuristic construction, and open problem. A proof-script
stub, numerical search, or secondary summary may raise review priority, but it
does not raise confidence without a cited source or inspectable artifact.
